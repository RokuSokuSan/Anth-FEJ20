Tribute to Eddie Van Halen

Edward "Eddie" Van Halen, ursprungligen Edward Lodewijk van Halen, född 26 januari 1955 i Nijmegen, Nederländerna,

är en amerikansk gitarrist som var sologitarrist i amerikanska hårdrocksgruppen Van Halen.

Wolf Van Halen, Eddies son

Edward "Eddie" Van Halen, lämnade oss den 6: october 2020 efter en tids sjukdom.

Eddie kämpade mot cancer i halsen. Wolf Van Halen, Eddies son, skrev:

“I can’t believe I’m having to write this, but my father, Edward Lodewijk Van Halen,

has lost his long and arduous battle with cancer this morning,” Wolfgang posted.

“He was the best father I could ever ask for.

>Every moment I share with him on and off stage was a gift.

My heart is broken and I don’t think I’ll ever fully recover from this loss.

I love you so much, Pop.”

We will miss you with all of our heart.

Such a spirit. He just could not live whithout the music. Thank you!
